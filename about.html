About Us

Recently under new management, the Black Dog Inn is a family-run pub and restaurant in the heart of Yorkshire, in the village of Camblesforth.

We offer a full range of tasty meals on our menu, all cooked fresh on the premises. Our Sunday Roast is always recommended and complimented.

Why not take a look at our menus, or contact us and book your table now. We always recommend booking a table, especially on a Sunday, but can always accommodate.

Make sure you keep checking back to our site, as we update it regularly with special events. Call 01757 618 777 to book your table now, although we can always accommodate.
