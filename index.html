A Warm Welcome To The Black Dog Inn

Hello and welcome to The Black Dog Inn, Camblesforth. Now under new management, we’re a family owned public house and restaurant situated in the heart of Yorkshire just a few miles south of York and Selby.

Whether you’re planning a day out, walking through the glorious local countryside or are simply passing, why not pop-in and sample our home cooked meals and range of drinks.

We have a large, beautiful beer garden available and are happy to cater for parties.
